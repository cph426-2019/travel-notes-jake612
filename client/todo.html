My Copenhagen To-do List

Here is a list of what I'd like to do in Copenhagen during my trip. Hopefully I'll get around to it all!

  1. Take a bike trip through the city
  2. Visit La Fontaine, Copenhagen's oldest jazz club
  3. See Christiansborg Palace, the Danish capitol building
  4. Make friends with a hippie at Freetown Christiania
  5. Ride a ride at Tivoli Gardens, one of the world's oldest amusement park
  6. See the painted houses at Nyhavn
  7. Admire the statues at the Glyptotek
  8. Climb to the top of the Rundetaarn
  9. Go swimming in a harbor bath
  10. Visit the Copenhagen Zoo
